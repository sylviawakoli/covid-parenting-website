Generali - The Human Safety Net Global Parenting Digital Hackathon

The Generali Group and our partner The Human Safety Net are organizing a 2-day global hackathon, mobilizing its Generali volunteering community in 22 countries to adapt the COVID-19 Parenting content in more than 20 languages. Together we hope that millions more vulnerable families will be able to benefit from this valuable support.
