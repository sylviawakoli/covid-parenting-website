TIPS FOR CASEWORKERS

We have designed special parenting tip sheets to be used by social workers for behavioral parent training, to help parents build positive parent-child relationships through play, reinforce positive and managing difficult behaviours, create structure and routines, talk about COVID-19, keep children safe online, and reduce stress and conflict.


If you can't find a caseworker tip sheet available in your language, please send us an email at: translate@covid19parenting.com


Please note that these translations were not created by the World Health Organization (WHO), and WHO is not responsible for the content or accuracy of these translations.
